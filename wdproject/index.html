The Seven

We make dreams come true

The Seven is the most popular and powerful American superhero team owned by Vought International. With Homelander being the only Supe on the team who was directly created by Vought, every other member of The Seven, excluding Black Noir, was raised outside of Vought's control.

While The Seven are believed to operate as a superhero team and stop crimes, in reality, every action they do is only fuel to increase Vought's stock prices and glorify their image with the public.
